## Key Messaging Points to Emphasize

### Pain Points (Mirror Their Language)
- "Customers hate QR ordering apps - they're slow and confusing"
- "Staff shortage means you can't handle peak hours"
- "Training new staff takes forever when turnover is high"
- "Language barriers with tourists/expats create mistakes"
- "Menu changes daily but updating apps is a hassle"
- "High-maintenance customers (allergies, special requests) eat up staff time"

### Solution Benefits (Outcomes, Not Features)
- "Orders in under 2 minutes" (not "fast")
- "Works in customer's language automatically" (not "multilingual AI")
- "No app download - uses Messenger" (not "web-based platform")
- "Pay per order, not monthly" (not "credit-based system")
- "Staff approves with one tap" (not "approval workflow")

### Social Proof (Once You Have It)
- "[Restaurant Name] increased orders by 30% in week 1"
- "Staff at [Restaurant] say they save 2 hours per shift"
- "98% of customers prefer OrderOctopus over traditional QR apps"

### Urgency (Without Being Pushy)
- "Only 3-5 spots for MVP"
- "Early bird pricing locks in low rates"
- "First restaurants get priority features"
